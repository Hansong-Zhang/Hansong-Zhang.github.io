About Me

I am a third-year PhD student at Institute of Information Enginering, Chinese Academy of Sciences in Beijing (2021-Present), advised by Prof. Shiming Ge. Before that, I got my Bachelor degree in School of Mathematics and Physics, North China Electric Power University (2017-2021), advised by Prof. Lei Wang.

My research interests include deep learning, computer vision and data-centric AI. Recently I am working on Learning from Crowds/Learning from multiple annotators and Dataset Distillation/Condensation.
